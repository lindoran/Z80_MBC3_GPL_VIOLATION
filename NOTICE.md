## Suspect Firmware Evidence

The files `EVIDENCE/binaries/suspect_IOS-S40427.hex` and
`EVIDENCE/binaries/suspect_IOS-S40427.bin` are preserved as evidence of the
distributed Z80-MBC3 IOS firmware. This repository does not claim original
authorship of those files. They are included to document and verify the apparent
GPLv3 compliance issue described in `REPORT/Z80_MBC3_GPL_VIOLATION.md`.
